What I Learned

One Conversation. A Clear Picture.

Our discovery session on 30 March gave me a detailed understanding of where Quad Lock is today and where you want to go.

The Business

  • 100+ people with ~90 in Melbourne and distributed teams across UK, Europe, China, and the U.S.
  • Globally recognised consumer brand with distribution in 100+ countries, strong e-commerce presence, and a product people genuinely love
  • Google Workspace environment with Claude and ChatGPT used interchangeably across the business
  • Mixed AI maturity. Some team members already building apps and agents. Others using AI for basic tasks. No unified strategy connecting the two

The Challenges

No single source of truth

Andrew described it clearly: everyone talks about the same number through a different lens. Inclusive of this, exclusive of that. Metrics differ by system, by team, by region. AI can unify this, but only with the right data architecture and governance underneath it.

Experimentation without strategy

People across the business are using AI, but independently. No shared prompt libraries. No way to build on each other's discoveries. No governance framework guiding what's safe and what's not. Everyone starting from scratch.

Distributed team complexity

Melbourne is the centre of gravity, but teams in the UK, Europe, China, and the U.S. need to be part of the transformation. Rollout has to be sequenced thoughtfully to maintain momentum without overwhelming remote teams.

Competitive pressure is real

If your peers announce 30% headcount reductions powered by AI, the cost pressure on Quad Lock becomes immediate. The best defence is to be the ones setting the pace, not reacting to it.

What Makes This Opportunity Different

Quad Lock already has builders on the team. You have people who are not waiting for permission. They're experimenting, creating, and pushing the boundaries of what AI can do in their roles.

This program doesn't start from zero. It takes what's already working, scales it across the organisation, fills the gaps with governance and strategy, and turns isolated pockets of 10% gains into company-wide 25%+ productivity lifts.

We're not teaching you to use AI. We're making Quad Lock the AI-native leader in your category.
