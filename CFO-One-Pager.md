### Recaptured Capacity (Conservative Model)

80 knowledge workers × 3 hours/week saved × $50/hr blended rate × 52 weeks

= **$624,000 per year** in recaptured capacity.

This assumes a 7.5% time recovery. Benchmarks from prior engagements (Freedom, PepsiCo) have delivered materially higher. This is the floor, not the ceiling.

---

### Benchmark ROI

**3-4x return on program spend within 12 months** is the conservative benchmark used across prior engagements. Validated at:

- **Freedom Furniture** — tracked ROI across multi-role retail AI activation, hours to minutes on reporting workflows, adoption sustained post-program
- **PepsiCo ANZ** — ~3x ROI, 5 functions engaged (Leadership, Legal, Procurement, Finance, Planning), 30-50% productivity uplift on targeted workflows
- **Business Events Perth** — whole-of-org activation for 40 people, 10-20% measurable productivity lift within weeks

Case studies and named referees available on request.

### Risk Reversal: Phase 0 Decision Gate

Quad Lock commits only to the **$10,000 Phase 0** in the first instance.

At the end of Week 2, if Phase 0 has not delivered clear, measurable value, the engagement stops. No obligation to proceed to Phase 1-3. The remaining $75,000 is fully conditional on Phase 0 outcomes.

**Effective worst-case exposure: $10,000.**

---

### Measurement Framework

Tracked weekly from Phase 0, reported at the end of each phase:

1. **Hours saved** per role and per function (time-and-motion sampling, not self-report)
2. **External spend reduced** (legal, agency, tooling redundancy, contractor hours)
3. **Workflow outputs shipped** (agents, Gems, prompts, standardised processes)
4. **Adoption depth** (percentage of team actively using AI in daily workflow)
5. **Revenue per head trajectory** (leading indicator, reviewed quarterly)

A single source of truth dashboard is built in Phase 1 and handed over in Phase 3.

### Why Now

- Quad Lock already has advanced builders in marketing and supply chain. Finance and People & Capability are early stage. Without a systematic framework, the capability gap between functions widens every month.
- The invoice verification agent proves the model works. The question is how to replicate that result across every function, not whether AI can deliver value here.
- Program is designed for **internal self-sufficiency**. The goal is that Quad Lock runs this without external support by end of Phase 3. No ongoing retainer.
- May 2026 start is currently held open. Subject to availability beyond this window.
